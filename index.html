The Story

A Race Against Extinction

A riveting, spoiler-light journey through the most ambitious solo mission humanity has ever launched.

The Sun Is Dimming

Scientists detect that the Sun is losing luminosity at an alarming rate. The culprit: Astrophage, a single-celled photosynthetic organism that feeds directly on stellar energy. Earth has perhaps thirty years before an ice age ends civilization.

The Hail Mary Mission

One star — Tau Ceti, 12 light-years away — shows no dimming. Humanity's last hope is to send a small crew there to discover why, then bring the answer back. The mission is a one-way trip for its crew.

A Man Wakes Up Alone

Ryland Grace regains consciousness aboard the Hail Mary with no memory. His two crewmates are dead. Piecing together fragments of memory, he slowly reconstructs who he is, why he is the only one left, and what he must do.

First Contact

Grace discovers he is not alone in the Tau Ceti system. An alien spacecraft from the planet Erid is conducting the same investigation. Against all odds, he establishes communication with the Eridian he calls Rocky — changing everything.

Two Worlds, One Problem

Working across species, language barriers, and wildly different biologies, Grace and Rocky pool their science to understand Astrophage. Their unlikely friendship becomes the story's beating heart.

The Choice

With a solution in hand, Grace must make an impossible decision — one that defines what it truly means to be human, and to be alive in an indifferent universe.

The Characters

Crew & Companions

Two beings from different stars, united by science — and something more.

🧑‍🔬
Ryland Grace
Protagonist · Astronaut & Scientist

A junior high school science teacher turned reluctant hero. Grace's boundless curiosity, self-deprecating humor, and deep love of science carry the reader across the void. His amnesia-driven self-discovery is one of fiction's great narrative engines.

🪐
Rocky
Eridian · Engineer & First Contact

An ammonium-breathing, five-limbed creature from a high-gravity world who communicates through musical tones. Rocky is one of science fiction's most beloved alien characters — curious, loyal, and every bit as funny as his human counterpart.

👩‍⚕️
Eva Stratt
Mission Commander · Earth

The cold, pragmatic European official granted unprecedented authority over every nation on Earth. She makes the ruthless decisions no one else will. Without her, the Hail Mary never launches.

🌟
Astrophage
Organism · The Existential Threat

A microscopic, single-celled life form that photosynthesizes stellar radiation and reproduces at an exponential rate. Part villain, part miracle of biology — Astrophage blurs the line between catastrophe and wonder.
